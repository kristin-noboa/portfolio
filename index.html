About Me

Hi, I'm Kristin! I'm a creative professional with hands-on experience in both digital and physical formats. No matter the medium, I lead with passion and pride in my work.

From the moment I was born, my mother knew I was a chatterbox. Throughout my life I've learned how to channel that talent into rapport building and networking. I graduated high school at age 17 with High Honors and Distinction. From there I spent time traveling the East Coast of the States, gathering both friends and skills in my professional life.

My most recent achievement includes returning to higher education at Seneca College. With a current 3.8 GPA, I've honed my digital craft through Adobe Suite applications while studying Interactive Media Design.

I'm always excited to meet new people and enhance my understanding of different world-views. Let's make something great together!
